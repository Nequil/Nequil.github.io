About Charles Coglizer

Some quirky and interesting information about me.

Childhood - Highschool

My name is Charles Coglizer but my friends and family call me CJ. I was born in SpringField, Missouri on December, 23 1997 too my parents Chuck and Terri Coglizer. My dad was the person that sparked my love for tech and video games, which my mom hated. When I was 10 my family moved to Georgia for my dads job. I was homeschooled since we where not sure if we would stay there. Well 12 years later where still here and was homeschooled the whole time. In 2015-2016 I got really into CSGO, I was actully quite good at it. I placed ranked 2000 in the North American region, and won a couple of lans in my area. It became apperant that I was not going to become a CSGO pro so I quit the 40 hours a week I was comitting to it which was definitely affecting my grades.

Adult Hood

In fall of 2016 I started college at Dalton State in Dalton Georgia. I had always wanted to be a Computer Scientist so I could work for Steve Jobs, even though he had died he still inspired me to become one. I'm still currently at Dalton State and getting ready to transfer in the spring of 2019. I will be doing P vs NP research in the fall of 2018 with Dr. Michael Hilgemann which I hope will help the research community in some way.
